Nelson Mandela

Anti-Apartheid Leader

Nelson Mandela was the president of South Africa from 1994-1999.
He was fighting against apartheid laws, which are "Jim Crow" laws.
He was put into prison for 27 years because he was described as trying to overthrow the government, when really he was trying to fight against the racism in South Africa.
His foundation was made to be against povert and porviding awareness with HIV/AIDS.
